Lasagna

A beautiful lasagna fresh from the oven

A wondferful, and with a few tricks relativley simple dish. It brings lots of flavor and versatility to the table if you arent too concenrend with keeping it authentic

With plenty of hearty meat sauce and mozarella cheese this easy italian favorite is sure to please. With only a few hours in the oven youll be enjoying the best thing you've had all week

Ingredients

  • 1LB ground beef
  • 1LB sausage Two 16oz cans of tomato
  • Two cups beef broth
  • Four Cloves of Garlic
  • One small white onion
  • One Package of Lasagna noodles
  • One bay leaf
  • Itialian Seasoning to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
