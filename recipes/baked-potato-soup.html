Baked Potato Soup

A hot bowl of Baked Potato Soup right out of the pot

A super easy and delicious stove top (or crock pot) meal that can be thrown together in no time

With just a few fresh, simple and mostly healthy ingredients youll be on your way to happiness

that can be stored in the fridge for a few days or frozen to last up to a couple of months!

Ingredients

  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 2/3 cups of margarine
  • 2/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 7 cups of milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 Green onion, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
